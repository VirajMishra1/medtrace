## Architectural Notes

### Cross-model compatibility
The pre-computed ICD-10 embeddings are 1536-dim and from an unknown model.
nomic-embed-text-v1.5 produces 768-dim embeddings.
**Strategy**: Re-embed top ~10,000 ICD-10 descriptions with nomic-embed-text-v1.5 during index building
for cross-model consistency. This ensures patient note embeddings and ICD-10 embeddings live in the same vector space.

### Patient data size
167K patient notes at ~500 tokens avg = ~83M tokens total.
At runtime, only the pre-indexed subset (~15K patients) is loaded.

### Index files to build
- `indexes/patient_faiss.index` — FAISS IndexFlatIP, 768-dim (nomic-embed-text)
- `indexes/patient_metadata.json` — list of {patient_uid, age, gender, snippet}
- `indexes/patients_subset.parquet` — subsampled patient dataframe
- `indexes/bm25_index.pkl` — rank_bm25 BM25Okapi, 15K patient notes
- `indexes/icd10_faiss.index` — FAISS IndexFlatIP, 768-dim (nomic-embed-text re-embedded)
- `indexes/icd10_mapping.json` — index → {code, description}
